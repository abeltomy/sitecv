WOOF-ing in Switzerland

Summer of 2023, when I was walking the Camino de Santiago and having long conversations with Chammie ,HK I came to know about WOOFing. So right away I got hooked and set up a profile in https://wwoof.net/ for Switzerland. I send a couple of requests to hosts, and one family in Oberhelfenschwil, Dorf, a small village near St. Gallen, was ready to host me.

Team BiohofBild

What is WOOFing?

  • Live with a host family and help with farm work.
  • No money exchanged; hosts provide accommodation and meals.
  • Gain hands-on experience.
  • Meet local people, make friends, and connect with the land.
